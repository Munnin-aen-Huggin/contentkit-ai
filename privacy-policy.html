Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited personal information to operate ContentKit AI and deliver purchased products:

  • Email address — collected via our opt-in form (powered by Kit/ConvertKit) when you request the free lead magnet or purchase a product.
  • Payment information — processed securely by Lemon Squeezy (our merchant of record) and Stripe. We do not store your card details on our servers.
  • Usage data — we may use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use our website. No individually identifiable browsing data is retained by us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver purchased digital products electronically after payment is confirmed.
  • To send the free lead magnet PDF you requested via our opt-in form.
  • To send marketing emails, including our nurture sequence and product announcements — you can unsubscribe at any time via the link at the bottom of every email.
  • To process payments and issue refunds via Lemon Squeezy and Stripe.
  • To comply with legal and tax obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate ContentKit AI. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Kit (ConvertKit) — email marketing platform used to store subscriber email addresses, deliver lead magnets, and send marketing sequences. See kit.com/privacy.
  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and merchant of record. Handles checkout, payment data, VAT, and digital product delivery. See lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.
  • Stripe — underlying payment infrastructure used by Lemon Squeezy. See stripe.com/privacy.
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting. Your IP address may be logged by GitHub per their privacy policy. See GitHub Privacy Statement.
  • Google Fonts — we load fonts from Google's CDN, which may set cookies and log your IP address per Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Ads (Google Ads/gtag.js) — we use Google Ads conversion tracking to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns. Google may set cookies on your browser to track conversions and serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ad Settings. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook) — we use the Meta Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our Facebook and Instagram advertising. Meta may set cookies to track website activity and serve relevant ads. You can manage your ad preferences at Facebook Ad Preferences. See Meta Privacy Policy.

4. Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe — You can unsubscribe from all marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in the footer of every email we send.
  • Data deletion — You can request deletion of your personal data by emailing support@contentkit.ai. We will process your request within 30 days.
  • EU residents (GDPR) — You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data. You also have the right to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
  • California residents (CCPA) — You have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Cookies

ContentKit AI does not set first-party cookies. Third-party services we use may set cookies per their own policies:

  • Google Ads (gtag.js) — sets cookies (including _gcl_aw and _gac) to track ad clicks, measure conversions, and serve personalized ads. These cookies typically expire after 90 days.
  • Meta Pixel — sets cookies (including _fbp and _fbc) to track website activity, measure ad performance, and enable retargeting. These cookies typically expire after 90 days.
  • Plausible Analytics — privacy-friendly analytics that does not use cookies and does not collect personal data.
  • Google Fonts & Lemon Squeezy — may set cookies per their own policies.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Opting out of Google personalized ads: Google Ad Settings. Opting out of Meta ads: Facebook Ad Preferences.

6. Data Retention

  • Your email address is retained in Kit (ConvertKit) for as long as you remain subscribed. Upon unsubscribe or deletion request, your data is removed from our email platform.
  • Payment records are retained by Lemon Squeezy and Stripe for as long as required by applicable tax, accounting, and legal regulations (typically 7 years).

7. Children's Privacy

ContentKit AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our website or services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact
