## Production Notes

1. All images should feel premium and dark â€” consistent with the getcontentkit.com brand
2. Avoid stock photography. Use text-focused designs, mockups, and gradients
3. Text should be readable at mobile sizes (no text smaller than 14px on 1080x1080)
4. Test all images at 50% scale to simulate mobile feed appearance
5. Include the blue-purple gradient accent in every image for brand consistency
6. Logo placement: bottom-left or bottom-center, never obstructing key text
