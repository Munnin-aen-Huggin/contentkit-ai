## Testing Framework

### Week 1-2: Creative Testing
- Run all ad creatives with equal budget split
- Identify top 2 performers per campaign by CTR and CPA

### Week 3-4: Audience Testing
- Test winning creatives against new audience segments
- Test lookalike audiences (1%, 3%, 5%) based on purchasers (after 100+ purchases)

### Week 5+: Scale
- Increase budget on winning ad set/creative combos by 20% every 3-4 days
- Kill any ad set with CPA > $75 after 3+ days
- Duplicate winning ad sets with fresh creatives to prevent ad fatigue
